About Us

MUSIC PRODUCTION FOR
INDEPENDENT ARTISTS

Konnect Studios is a professional recording studio located in Islington, London, featuring the best of both vintage analog and modern digital tools. Music is our passion, and we focus on making a big sound in an intimate setting.

Recording

Book a session with one of our engineers and have your project processed to your specific needs. Bring your band or instrumental track-out, we work with all genres of music.

Production

Work with our producers to have your instrumental composed to your unique sound. We build the beat from scratch or remake an instrumental of your choice in a one on one session.
